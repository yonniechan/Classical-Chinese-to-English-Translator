Tsze-lu got Tsze-kao appointed governor of Pi.The Master said, 'You are injuring a man's son.'Tsze-lu said, 'There are (there) common people and officers;there are the altars of the spirits of the land and grain. Why mustone read books before he can be considered to have learned?'The Master said, 'It is on this account that I hate yourglib-tongued people.'
